# Module 0: Orientation and Course Setup

Welcome to the course orientation! Before we build systems, we must prepare our learning environment, understand the course philosophy, and map out our candidate use cases.

---

## 🎯 Module Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:
1. Understand the practical, documentation-first philosophy of this course.
2. Initialize your local Git/Markdown workspace and link your files.
3. Identify three business friction candidate use cases to choose from.
4. Establish your personal learning log to track skill transformation.
5. Define baseline metrics and success criteria for your learning journey.
