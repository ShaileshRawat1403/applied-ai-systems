# Template 16: Governance Pack

The Governance Pack is the official compliance document detailing the risk profile, access boundaries, human oversight model, and system control checks of your AI system. It is designed for review by security teams, legal counsels, and compliance officers.

---

```yaml
owner_department: "Operations & Communications"

```
