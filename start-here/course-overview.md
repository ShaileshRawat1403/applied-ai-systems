# Course Overview

Welcome to **Applied AI Systems: From Prompt to Production**. This page outlines the core syllabus, module objectives, and time commitments for your learning journey.

---

## 📅 Course Curriculum

The course is structured into 9 sequential modules and a final Capstone project, totaling **40 to 60 hours** of practical effort.

| Module | Title | Primary Output | Estimated Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Module 0** | Orientation and Course Setup | Setup workspace & Candidates list | 2 Hours |
| **Module 1** | Applied AI Foundations | AI Opportunity Map | 4 Hours |
| **Module 2** | From Business Friction to AI Use Case | AI Opportunity Brief | 4 Hours |
| **Module 3** | Prompt to Specification | Prompt Specification Pack | 6 Hours |
| **Module 4** | Knowledge Systems and RAG | RAG Readiness & Retrieval Design | 6 Hours |
| **Module 5** | Tools, APIs, and Workflow Integration | Tool Permission Map | 6 Hours |
| **Module 6** | Agentic Workflows and Orchestration | Agent Workflow Canvas | 6 Hours |
| **Module 7** | Evaluation, Debugging, and Observability| Evaluation Harness Plan | 6 Hours |
| **Module 8** | Security, Governance, and Human Review | Governance & Risk Pack | 6 Hours |
| **Module 9** | Deployment, Adoption, and Capstone | Final AI Workflow Pilot Pack | 8 Hours |

---

## 🏆 Key Student Transformation

* **Before the Course**: You know some AI tool names and write random prompts, but you struggle with hallucinations, security questions, formatting errors, or demonstrating ROI to your manager.
* **After the Course**: You can confidently identify a high-value AI opportunity, architect an end-to-end workflow, write strict prompt specs, implement human verification gates, test performance programmatically, and build a compelling business case to deploy your pilot.
